FAQ

Questions answered.

How do I get my catalog after paying? +
After your M-Pesa payment is confirmed your PDF is generated instantly. Download it from the order status page — the link never expires.
Can I see a preview before paying? +
Yes. After filling the form you get a partial watermarked preview of your actual catalog before any payment is required.
What image formats work for product photos? +
JPG, PNG, and WEBP. For best results use clear well-lit photos. Images are automatically optimized for the PDF.
Can I update my catalog later? +
Each order generates one catalog. To update prices or products simply create a new order — it only takes a few minutes.
I paid but can't find my catalog. What do I do? +
Use the Track Order section — enter your phone number to find all your orders. If still stuck, WhatsApp us with your M-Pesa receipt and we'll sort it in 30 minutes.
