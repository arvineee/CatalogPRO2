FAQ

Got questions?
We have answers.

How do I get my catalog after paying?
+
The moment your M-Pesa payment is confirmed, your PDF catalog is generated instantly. You're redirected to your download page automatically — no waiting, no emails required. The download link never expires.
Can I see a preview before paying?
+
Yes, always. After you fill the form, you see a real watermarked preview of your actual catalog pages before any payment is required. What you preview is exactly what you'll download.
What image formats work for product photos?
+
JPG, PNG, and WEBP are all supported. For best results, use clear, well-lit photos. Images are automatically resized and optimized for your PDF catalog.
Can I update my catalog later?
+
Each order generates one catalog. To update prices or products, simply create a new order — the form saves your previous data automatically, so it only takes a few minutes to update.
I paid but can't find my catalog. Help!
+
Use the "Track Order" section above — enter your phone number to find all your orders and re-download anytime. If still stuck, WhatsApp us with your M-Pesa receipt and we'll sort it within 30 minutes.
Can I share the catalog on WhatsApp and social media?
+
Absolutely — that's the whole point. Your catalog is a standard PDF that works on all devices. Share it on WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, print it, email it, or upload it to your website.
